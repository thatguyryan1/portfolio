Anthropocene

2022 | Editorial Design, Collaborative Project | Tools: InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop

Anthropocene is a collaborative project exploring human impact on Earth’s ecosystems. Out of the book’s five sections, I worked on the forest fires portion. While I initially focused on the destructive nature of fires, I eventually shifted the concept to highlight rebirth, reflecting how natural fires play a vital role in many ecosystems. Each team member designed their section independently, but the team responded so positively to my style that the other sections adapted their designs to match, giving the book a cohesive visual identity. Beyond the book, the project included additional products, such as stickers; I designed mine inspired by the phoenix, a symbol of death and rebirth, tying directly into my section’s theme.
