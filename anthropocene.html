Anthropocene

Anthropocene is a group project about human’s impact on earth’s ecosystem. Starting off as a book which was split into five sections, the section I chose was forest fires. Initially starting as a section about destruction as fires are usually seen as, eventually developed into a section more about rebirth which natural fires are seen as in many ecosystems. Major design elements such as the style of the title page for each section as well as the layout styles of some pages were created by myself and were replicated in other sections. Other products besides the book were also created such as stickers where each section created their own which I designed mine after a phoenix, a mythological creature known for death and rebirth.
