Promotional Trailer Concept

CN Classics

2023 | Book Design, VHS Product & Advertisement | Tools: InDesign, Photoshop, Premiere Pro

This project explored nostalgia through my childhood love of Cartoon Network. I investigated what made these cartoons different from modern shows, focusing on their use of cell animation, which I documented in a book detailing Cartoon Network’s history. I then created a product presenting these cartoons in a VHS format, designing a custom box and producing an accompanying advertisement. Transferring the ad from computer to tape was challenging, requiring research and specific wiring to preserve quality. To enhance authenticity, I added era-accurate tape imperfections and visual distortions, letting viewers experience the cartoons as they would have been watched during that era.
