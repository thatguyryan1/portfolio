The World

A 500 page book created with the theme of being major attributes of what makes a villain truly villain. Tragic villains are something I always found more interesting than heroes as a child and is something I wanted to explore in this project. The book has seven different sections involving a different attribute to describe a villain. Throughout the book, the reader follows the main character in an apocalyptic world who slowly turns into a villain as a small decision which could have developed the character into a hero slowly turns the character into what is considered a tragic villain. Each of these decisions is then displayed by a flipbook element with scenes from major movies which had been converted into text using OCR to fit the theme of the apocalyptic world.
