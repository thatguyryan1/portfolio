The World

2022 | Book Design, Narrative Development | Tools: InDesign, Photoshop, Image-to-Text Generator

This 500-page book dives into what makes a villain, focusing on tragedy over heroism. It follows a character in an apocalyptic world whose small choice—one that would have set them down the path of a hero—leads them on a dark, tragic course. Each turning point is paired with a flipbook element made from iconic movie scenes. I converted these images into text using an image-to-text generator, reinforcing the book’s distressed, apocalyptic style while exploring the influence of technology on our future.
