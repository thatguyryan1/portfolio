Simera Festival

2023 | Graphic Design, Client Collaboration | Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop

Simera Festival was a collaborative project in which one team member acted as the client while the other served as the designer. After conducting an interview to understand my client’s interests, I created a poster inspired by 1970s music festivals that reflected their passion for live music. I used their favorite color, orange, as the foundation for a retro-inspired color palette, capturing the era’s vibrant aesthetic. To personalize the design, I incorporated their favorite artists and key facts about them into the background, creating a poster that was both visually engaging and uniquely tailored to the client’s identity.
