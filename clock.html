Clock

A clock that requires constant cranking to accurately tell the time. Essentially, you must spend all your time to know what the time is. Created in Blender, with the intent to be 3D printed however every attempt has resulted in the print being messed up with only parts of it being fully completed. Because it couldn’t be printed, an animation was then created to at least display how it might work.
