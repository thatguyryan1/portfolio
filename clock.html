Clock

2023 | 3D Modeling, Conceptual Design & Animation | Tools: Blender, Animation

This project explores time as a resource through a clock that requires constant cranking to function, meaning you must spend all your time to know the time. Created during my first experience using Blender, I modeled the clock with the intent to 3D print it. While the prints were only partially successful, I produced an animation to fully illustrate how the mechanism would operate. The project demonstrates both conceptual design and my ability to learn and apply new 3D modeling tools.
