4STRONG PAWS

2025 | Web Design & Development, Branding | Tools: WordPress, Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML/CSS, Javascript

I independently designed and developed a website for a veterinary client from scratch, guiding them through multiple design iterations and providing options at each stage to ensure their vision was represented. I also created a new logo, iterating several versions based on their original design before finalizing the brand identity. The site was fully built on WordPress after migrating from GoDaddy, tested for functionality, and optimized for mobile accessibility and responsiveness across devices. By building the site through WordPress instead of GoDaddy’s site builder, I helped the client save money on the higher costs of GoDaddy’s website creation tools and maintenance plans. Throughout the project, I communicated updates regularly, incorporating client feedback to refine the final design, resulting in a final product the client was very pleased with.
