Burnin' For You

Originally starting out as a simple project where we were supposed to design an album cover for a song. The song I chose was ‘Burnin for you’ by Blue Oyster Cult which I used the meaning behind the lyrics to the song for my inspiration in my initial design. After creating the product however, I felt a need to create a continuation to the design and thus created a lyric video in after effects as a simple album design didn’t feel enough.
