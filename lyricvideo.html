Burnin' For You

2021 | Graphic Design, Motion Graphics | Tools: Photoshop, After Effects

For this project, I selected the song “Burnin’ for You” by Blue Öyster Cult as the basis for an album cover design. Drawing from the lyrical themes, I developed a visual concept that reflected the song’s meaning. The design of a clock with legs was incorporated to symbolize the interplay of desire and the passage of time, inspired by the lyrics’ themes of enduring passion. After completing the cover, I expanded the project into a lyric video created in After Effects, extending the design into motion and exploring how the visuals could enhance the listening experience.
